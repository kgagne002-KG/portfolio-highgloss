Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about working with a freelance web developer in Ontario.

How much does a website cost for a small business in Ontario?

Website pricing depends on features, pages, and functionality. Small business websites in Ontario typically range from basic one-page sites to fully custom multi-page builds designed for growth.

Do I need a custom website or can I use a template?

A custom website offers better performance, SEO, and flexibility compared to templates. It allows your business to stand out and scale over time.

What is included in SEO-optimized website design services?

SEO-optimized websites include proper page structure, fast load speeds, mobile responsiveness, and content built around relevant search keywords.

How long does it take to build a custom website?

Timelines vary depending on project scope. A simple site may take a few weeks, while larger business websites can take longer due to design, revisions, and content.

Do you offer web design services near St. Thomas Ontario?

Yes. KG Glam Designs provides web design services for businesses in St. Thomas, Ontario and surrounding areas, as well as remote clients.
