Team Showcase

A responsive layout for presenting people, roles, and supporting content with clear hierarchy and consistent spacing across screen sizes.

Primary Role

Demonstrates name hierarchy, supporting text length, and balanced spacing within a reusable card layout.

Secondary Role

Shows how longer descriptions wrap cleanly without disrupting the overall grid or visual rhythm.

Supporting Role

Illustrates how supporting information fits naturally without overpowering primary content.

Additional Role

The layout scales gracefully to accommodate varying numbers of entries while maintaining alignment.

This component is intended for use in about pages, internal directories, or dashboards where consistent presentation and responsiveness matter.
